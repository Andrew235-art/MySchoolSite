Admission Process

  1. Submit Application: Complete and submit the online application form or download the PDF form and submit it to the admissions office.
  2. Entrance Exam: Eligible candidates will be invited to take an entrance examination.
  3. Interview: Successful candidates from the entrance exam will be called for an interview.
  4. Offer of Admission: Admission offers will be sent to successful candidates.
  5. Enrollment: Complete the enrollment process by the specified deadline.

Requirements

  • Completed application form.
  • Previous academic transcripts.
  • Birth certificate.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
  • Recommendation letters (if applicable).
